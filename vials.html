Mounjaro Single-use Vial and Storage

Storing Mounjaro vials

  • Store all unopened vials in  the refrigerator at 36ºF to  46ºF(2ºC to 8ºC)
  • Do not freeze Mounjaro. If the vial has been frozen, DO NOT USE
  • Keep away from heat and out of direct light
  • Unopened vials can be used until the expiration date on the carton  and label if they have been stored
    in the refrigerator
  • Keep Mounjaro vials, syringes, needles, and all medicines out of the  reach of children
  • Unopened vials stored at room temperature should be thrown  away after 21 days

Before using a vial

  • Check the vial label to make sure that the medicine has not expired
  • Make sure the medicine is not frozen, is colorless to slightly yellow, is not cloudy,
    and does not have particles

After vials have been opened

  • Throwaway all opened vials after use, even if there is medicine left inside

Preparing to Use a Mounjaro Vial

Pick your day. Follow the steps

Follow the Instructions for Use included with your medicine.

Pick a day that works for you:

Take Mounjaro on the same day each week. It may help to set a reminder on your phone or calendar for this day. If you need to change the day, make sure there have been at least 3 days since your last injection

You will also need:

  • 2 alcohol swabs
  • A sharps container for throwing
  • Gauze
  • A syringe with needle, as away used needles and syringes prescribed by your doctor

Syringe With Needle

The device recommended by your healthcare provider may
look different from that shown above.

Disposal of syringes and needles

  • Throw away used needles and syringes in a sharps container
  • Your healthcare provider can tell you how to obtain and dispose of sharps containers
  • Do not recycle your used sharps container

How to Inject Mounjaro

Step-by-Step Instructions

1

Prepare the vial

  • Pull off the plastic protective cap, but do not remove the
    rubber stopper

  • Wipe the rubber stopper with an alcohol swab

2

Attach needle and syringe

  • Remove the outer wrapping from the syringe and needle

  • Place the needle on top of the syringe and turn until it is tight and firmly attached

3

Prepare the vial

  • Remove the needle shield by pulling it straight off

  • Hold the syringe with the exposed needle pointing up

  • Pull down on the plunger until the plunger tip reaches the line on the syringe indicating 0.5 mL

4

Prepare the vial

  • Push the needle through the rubber stopper of the vial

  • Push the plunger all the way in. This puts air in the vial

5

Prepare the vial

  • Turn the vial and syringe upside down

  • Slowly pull the plunger down until the plunger tip is past the 0.5-mL line

  • If there are air bubbles, tap the syringe gently a few times to let any air bubbles rise to the top

6

Prepare the vial

  • Slowly push the plunger up until the plunger tip reaches the 0.5-mL line

  • Pull the syringe out of the rubber stopper of the vial

7

Prepare the vial

  • Wipe your injection site of choice with an alcohol swab (let dry before injection)

  • You can inject Mounjaro into your stomach or thighs. Another person can give you the injection in the back of your upper arm

  • You can use the same part of your body each week, but choose a different injection site within that area

8

Prepare the vial

  • Insert the needle into your skin and push down on the plunger to inject your dose

  • The plunger tip should be at the bottom of the syringe, and the needle should stay in your skin for at least 5 seconds to make sure you have injected all of your medicine

  • Pull the needle out of your skin and place the needle directly into your sharps container

  • Do not recap the needle
